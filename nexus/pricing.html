Network Charging Structure

In Nigeria, different networks charge different unit rates per page of SMS (1 page = 160 characters).

MTN: 1 Unit / page
Airtel: 1 Unit / page
Glo: 1 Unit / page
9mobile: 1 Unit / page

Note: DND (Do Not Disturb) lines require special routing and are charged at 2 units per page across all networks.
