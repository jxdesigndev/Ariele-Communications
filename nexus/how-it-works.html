How Nexus delivers.

We stripped away the complexity of traditional bulk SMS and focused on what actually matters: guaranteed delivery.

The Technology

Intelligent Routing

When you hit send, Nexus doesn't just broadcast blindly. It analyzes network congestion across Nigeria in real-time and routes your messages through the fastest available channels to MTN, Airtel, Glo, and 9mobile.

DND Compliance & Override

Over 60% of Nigerian lines are on the NCC's Do Not Disturb list. As an approved transactional gateway, Nexus correctly flags your critical updates (like school emergencies or church notices) to legitimately bypass DND restrictions without violating regulations.

Using the Platform

1

Upload Contacts

Drop your Excel or CSV file into Nexus. We'll automatically clean the numbers and remove duplicates.

2

Compose Message

Type your message. We'll show you exactly how many SMS pages it consumes and total cost before you send.

3

Send or Schedule

Blast it instantly or schedule it for a specific date and time. Delivery reports update in real-time.
