THE ROLE

What Companions Do

As a GoodPal companion, you're matched with subscribers based on your expertise. You listen, you engage, you challenge politely, and you bring your real perspective.

Sessions last from 30 minutes to 2 hours. Your job is not to agree with everything they say, but to provide a genuinely interesting, intellectually honest conversation.

REQUIREMENTS

Who We're Looking For

  • Knowledgeable in 2+ specific topic areas
  • Strong conversational English
  • Reliable internet connection and quiet environment
  • Available for at least 5 hours of regular sessions per week
  • Genuinely curious about other people's perspectives
THE PROCESS

How It Works

1. Apply

This is real work.

Companions are vetted, trained, and paid. This is not a volunteer position.

  • Performance bonuses for high ratings
