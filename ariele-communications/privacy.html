Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Ariele Communications ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our parent website or use our products, including Nexus and GoodPal.

2. Nexus (B2B) Data Handling

For our Nexus platform, we process contact data (such as parent or congregant phone numbers) strictly on behalf of our client institutions. We do not sell, rent, or repurpose this data for our own marketing. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

3. GoodPal (B2C) Privacy

GoodPal conversations are strictly confidential. We do not record audio or video of sessions. We only retain metadata (duration, topics, ratings) necessary to facilitate billing and quality assurance.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@ariele-communications.com.
